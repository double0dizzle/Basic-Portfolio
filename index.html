About Me

I’m an eight-year Navy veteran with cryptologic experience in “decoding” within a tactical environment, and am now on the flip-side of that by building programs in the private sector. Programming excites me, and I’m a life-long dedicated student, regardless if I’m in an actual classroom or not. I’m looking forward to tackling challenges with a creative team and putting my skills to the test, and I believe both my military experience and Master’s degree are indicators of my commitment to hard-work and learning.

I have experience with front-end languages such as JavaScript, CSS, and HTML, as well as technologies such as Bootstrap, jQuery, JSON, Firebase, and Ajax.

Aside from coding, my hobbies including exploring mountain trails, playing sand volleyball, and biking about 5 times a week. I'm no stranger to the outdoors, or just enjoying the simpler things in life.
